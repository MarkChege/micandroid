Back when Ningbo was small, we all knew each other, and we knew where everything was and there was a great sense of community.

These days Ningbo is a huge model city with millions of residents and thousands of locations, exploring the city while staying connected to each other is a modern challenge.

It's like we're surrounded by everything and everyone we need in Ningbo but we're somehow not as connected as we used to be.

In fact we often stick to what we know. I mean think about it, there are thousands of places that we havent explored yet within Ningbo, not to mention the millions of fantastic people.

The problem is that we are lost in too many choices, So Ningbo is going to bring the places and the people of this great city together again.

You can find out more about us and our vision on our about us page.
